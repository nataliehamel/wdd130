Dry Oar Rafting

Our mission is to give our clients safe, thrilling, and unforgetable adventures! Our goal is to provide guides, tips, advice, and support for our clients. We want to help our clients become reliable, safe, and skilled rafters in hopes that they feel ready and prepared to take on more adventures in the future.

History

Our company has been around since 2015. We have serviced clients all over the country. We are currently located in West Virginia, Idaho, Oregon, Maine, Alaska, and Colorado. We got started up because of our enthusiasm for this sport. We absolutely love being in the wild waters all day! We bring the best possible skills and knowledge to help our clients every day. Feel free to contact us for more information!
