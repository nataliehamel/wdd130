The 2 Day Pack

The 2 Day Pack is a two day trip down the river. The first day consists of 10 hours and then you will camp at halfpoint for the night. The second day is another 8 hours. At the final destination a bus will be waiting to pick you up and transfer you and your rafts back to main camp.

The Full Day Pack

The Full Day Pack is a 15 hour trip down the river. At around 7 hours we pull off the rive and have lunch. After lunch it is another 8 hours to the final destination. There a bus will be waiting to transfer you and your raft back up to main camp.

The Family Pack

The Family Pack is a 3 hour trip down the river. It is calm and enjoyable for all ages. Children under 15 years old must be accompanied by an adult. Once to your final destination a bus will be waiting to take you and your family and supplies back to main camp.
