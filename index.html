California

California is a state on the western coast. It is bordered by the Pacific Ocean to the west, Oregon to the north, Nevada to the east, Arizona to the southeast, and Mexico to the south. California is know for its beautiful outdoors this is because it is home to the redwood forest, the Sierra Nevada mountains, the Mojave Desert, the central valley, and cliff-lined beaches. Some top destination points in California include the Golden Gate Bridge, Yosemite National Park, Hollywood, Disney Land, and Joshua Tree National Park.
